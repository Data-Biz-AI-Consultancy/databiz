CASE STUDY 01 • AI & AUTOMATION

Autonomous Lead Triage & Intelligent Operations

An innovative German ClimateTech scaleup (home insulation installer) was facing operational friction. As their inbound volume scaled to over 1,200 leads/month, manual triage became a bottleneck. With a typical project deal value of €5,000, the stakes were high: planning mistakes or mispriced quotes during the sales-to-operations handoff impacted 20% of projects, causing costly site rework and margin erosion.

HOW WE SOLVED IT

We architected and deployed a stateless, multi-stage AI qualification pipeline integrated directly into their CRM:

  • check_circle Rule Engine: Instant verification of strict municipal and structural criteria.
  • check_circle LLM Parser: Cross-checks structured intake forms against raw sales call transcripts to flags contradictions.
  • check_circle RAG Memory: Semantic lookup against 1,000+ past projects to identify identical property profiles and flag historical risks.

BUSINESS IMPACT

  • check_circle Lead onboarding productivity: Reclaims 300+ hours/mo of sales capacity by automating manual screening for 1,200+ leads.
  • check_circle Project risk reduction: Cuts planning and post-installation failures from 17% to <5%, protecting €36,000/mo in margin.

CASE STUDY 02 • MODERN INFRASTRUCTURE

MeshPilot — Enterprise Data Stack & Cost Control

An enterprise organization experienced critical data scaling pains. Compute costs on Snowflake were rising unpredictably, query execution was slowing down, and duplicate metrics across departments destroyed decision alignment.

HOW WE SOLVED IT

We implemented our proprietary **MeshPilot** enterprise data architecture framework:

  • check_circle Optimized Materialization: Configured incremental dbt models and partition keys to eliminate redundant cloud compute reads.
  • check_circle Quality Pyramid: Enforced foundational checks, referential integrity rules, and anomaly-detection alerts via Elementary.
  • check_circle Governance Alignment: Built clean role-based access control (RBAC) structures for safe data sharing across business domains.
